The Week's Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Acclimatization

Arrive and settle into your serene abode. The evening begins with a warm welcome dinner and a gentle ice-breaking session under the stars.

Day 2-6: Exploration & Enchantment

Your days will be filled with guided nature walks, visits to hidden lakes, cultural immersion, and optional adventures. Each evening is a celebration with live music by the bonfire.

Day 7: Farewell Sunrise

Witness one last glorious sunrise, enjoy a heartfelt farewell breakfast, and depart with a peaceful mind and a full heart.
